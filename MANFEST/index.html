ABOUT INSTITUTE

Bannari Amman Institute of Technology, established in 1996 by the Bannari Amman Group, serves as one of the citadels of higher education in the Southern part of India. BIT aids to impart knowledge, teamwork, innovation, entrepreneurship, courage, sacrifice and duty which are innards of a meaningful life. A serene place for learning, discovery, innovation, expression and discourse. The Institute caters to the needs of the students and members of faculty in realizing their dreams and aspirations. BIT has been conferred with a number of accolades and recognitions from top-notch forums of high eminence.

ABOUT DEPARTMENT

School of Management Studies (SMS) has been established in 2008 with a vision to be a center of excellence for developing corporate leaders who make a difference in the globally competitive market through their professional competence blended with due social concern and a high value system. The salubrious environment coupled with world-class infrastructure provides a wonderful setting for the members of faculty who are predominantly drawn from industry to impart the necessary hard and soft skills using the appropriate pedagogy.

ABOUT MANFEST

School of Management Studies organizes annual intercollegiate management meet ‘ManFest’ which bring together participants from various Arts, Science, Engineering and Management Students from various states. Events related to Team Building, Leadership and Analyzing are being conducted to give an exposure to the students the flavor of being competitive. We welcome all the students to come and explore the joy of togetherness.

GENERAL GUIDELINES

*The event is open for all B-schools, engineering and arts colleges

*Participants should possess their identity card issued by the respective institutes

*There is no limit to the number of team entries from an institution

*All the participants are required to report on time

*Since all events will happen simultaneously participant of a particular event would not be able to participate in other events

*All decisions regarding registration and participation is at the discretion of the organizers

*The decision of the jury panel is final in selecting the awardees

*In case of any dispute, decision of ManFest-2K20 organizing committee shall be final and binding

*Any damage to the college infrastructure or to the facilities provided to the participants will be treated as a serious offense and the cost of damage would be recovered from the concerned persons

*Teams are required to register for MANFEST’20 before 20:00 hours, February 25, 2020 @ www.manfest2k20.com

*Violation of rules and regulations would lead to disqualification and exit from the campus immediately

*There shall be no refunds of registration fee for any cause
